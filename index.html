About Mort

Morton Oliver Steinberg was born on May 14th, 1944 in Bakersfield, California. His mom, a single mother, worked weekends to make ends meet while raising him. Starting off in politics at the age of 18, he's been a loyal Libertarian for 56 years. Growing up, he was friends with Clint Eastwood, a famous actor/director, and a Libertarian. He marched with Dr. Martin Luther King Jr. (alongside long-time associate Sen. Bernie Sanders) in the March on Washington. Morty has served the American people for decades, and he hopes to continue to do so as a US Senator for California.
